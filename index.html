Mission

National Level Excellence and International Visibility in Every Facet of Engineering Research and Education.

Vision

To facilitate quality transformative education in Engineering and Management. To foster innovations in Technology and its application for meeting global challenges. To pursue and disseminate Quality Research. To equip, enrich and transform students to be Responsible Professionals for better service to humanity.

Accreditation Status

All B.Tech Programs are accredited by National Board of Accreditation till 30-06-2022.
B.Arch Program and M.Planning(Housing) is accredited by National Board of Accreditation till 30-06-2022.
MBA Programme is accredited by National Board of Accreditation till 30-06-2022.

Quality Policy

It is our commitment to impart Quality Engineering and Management Education to the students, acquiring advanced knowledge in Science and Technology, while continually striving to improve the effectiveness and efficiency of the system by periodically reviewing all processes, so as to enable our students to become Competent Professionals useful to the society.
